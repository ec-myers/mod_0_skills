Class: Menu Item

Attributes:

Name (string)
Description (string)
Price (integer)
Item Number (integer)
Ingredients (array)

Methods:

Cook_Menu_Item (References the ingredients attribute to prepare the correct menu item)

Add_To_Ticket (Uses the price attribute to add the item ordered to the guests ticket)

Modify_Item_Add (References the ingredients attribute and adds additional ingredients desired by the guest)

Modify_Item_remove(References the ingredients attribute and removes ingredients from the array that are not desired by the guest)
